About Us

Wild Rescues is a registered nonprofit wildlife rescue and rehabilitation facility, ocated in Ocala, Florida. We help injured, sick, neglected, and orphaned wildlife. We help rehabilitate these animals and then release them back into the wild, if possible.

Wild Rescues is a member of the National Wildlife Rehabilitators Association.

We help many animals, such as:

  • Raccoons
  • Squirrels
  • Foxes
  • Birds
  • Horses
  • Deer
  • Pigs
  • Reptiles

Have you found an injured or rophaned animal? Contact us to see if we can help.
